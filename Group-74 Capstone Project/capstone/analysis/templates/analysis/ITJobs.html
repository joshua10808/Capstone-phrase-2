Brisbane IT Jobs Listed Each Month

Here you will be able to view how many IT jobs have been listed each month. Feel free to hover each point on the line to view the exact number of jobs provided that month. Filters are also provided for further analysis.

General View shows the total amount of IT jobs provided per month.
Information Systems shows the amount of IT jobs provided per month that requires information systems knowledge ONLY.
Computer Science shows the amount of IT jobs provided per month that requires computer science knowledge ONLY.
Combination shows the total amount of IT jobs provided per month that requires both knowledge from information systems and computer science.

NOTE: As this website has only just been launched, the current chart will only show data from the year 2017. Job listed per month is the average of that month. No data are available prior to July 2017. As we collect more data in the future, further information will be presented on this chart. Last Updated: October 26, 2017.
